Groomsmen The Boys

Matt Kolbeck
Best Man

I've known Matt since we were awkward freshmen in high school. Aside from getting into the occasional mischief, we both played football and lacrosse in high school that helped solidify the man-bond. Even though we're 3000 miles apart, it only takes a little water to keep the garden growing. To prove he is truly my best man, Matt was on the receiving end of my first touchdown pass in high school.

Craig Middleton
College Roommate

I met Craig during my first week of college at Drew University. We were both lacrosse players and lived across the hall from each other. To prove that the world is indeed a small place, Craig and I actually met each other briefly when we were about 5 years old and didn't know until college. He is one of my closest friends - if you run into Craig at the wedding, ask him to tell some Drew stories!

Derek Haff
College Roommate

Derek has the best sense of humor of anyone I know. His ability to deliver jokes, as well as receive jokes, continues to astound me and leave me in tears. We got to live together for a year during our time at Drew in one of the coolest suites (or so we thought) on campus. He's an all around great guy who everyone loves - just don't trust him behind the wheel of a golf cart.

Future Brother-In-Law
